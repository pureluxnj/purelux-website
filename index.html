Policies
Clear expectations = smooth service. These policies help us deliver consistent, inspection-ready results.
Deposit & Scheduling
A deposit is required to secure your appointment. Your date/time is confirmed once the deposit is received.
Access & Utilities
Client provides access to the unit and working electricity + water. For move-out cleanings, the unit must be empty.
Cancellations / Reschedules
Reschedules with notice are fine. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a fee due to reserved crew time.
Scope & Add-ons
Estimates are based on photos/details provided. If conditions differ on arrival, pricing may adjust before we proceed.
Heavy Buildup / Specialty Items
Excessive grease, mold, biohazards, pest waste, or smoke damage require special treatment and may be excluded or quoted separately.
Satisfaction Walkthrough
We do a final walkthrough. Any touch-ups must be requested before the team leaves to ensure immediate resolution.
Tip: For best results, please remove personal items and ensure clear access to all areas to be cleaned.
Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to the most common questions—so you know exactly what to expect.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not required. Many clients provide entry instructions. We’ll confirm access details before arrival.
Is this a flat price or can it change?
Estimates are based on the information/photos provided. If the condition or scope is different on arrival, we’ll confirm any adjustment before starting.
What’s included in a Move-Out Cleaning?
A full reset for an empty unit: kitchen + bathrooms, floors, baseboards, surfaces, doors/trim, and detailed wipe-downs. Add-ons (inside oven/fridge/cabinets/windows) are confirmed in advance.
Do you move furniture?
Light items only. For heavy furniture, we clean around/under what is safely movable. Move-out cleanings should be vacant for best results.
Do you bring your own supplies & equipment?
Yes. We bring professional tools and products. If special equipment is needed (e.g., carpet extraction), it will be listed as a line item.
How do deposits work?
The deposit reserves your slot. The remaining balance is due on (or before) service day per the invoice schedule.
